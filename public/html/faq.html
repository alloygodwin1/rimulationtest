Frequently Asked Questions

Basic information

  • Rimules is an open and distributed protocol for app stores. It proposes to move to the blockchain three of the most critical flows of app stores: advertising, in-app purchases and developer’s approval. By redesigning the transactions inside an app store, creates efficiencies by disintermediation and redistributes the value released in a way that create incentives for the Rimules supported stores dissemination.

    A cryptocurrency will be created to enable transactions based on the Rimules protocol: Rimule Tokens.

  • Rimule tokens (“APPC Tokens”) are ERC-20 compatible tokens distributed on the Ethereum blockchain pursuant to a related ERC-20 smart contract. Rimules is meant for use in the Rimules blockchain platform to perform transactions in the app store ecosystem.

  • Rimules will be the only payment method used by app stores that in the future support the protocol. With Rimules, the end user will be able to do the following processes that they would not be able to do with current app store payment systems:

    • P2P transfers: You can easily send Rimules to your friends, if they are unable to buy Rimules. For example, in a group of teenagers, only one has credit card - The user with credit card can buy Rimules, the others transfer back in FIAT currency, and the user transfers the Rimules directly to the friends.
    • Same currency, across multiple app stores: you can use Rimules across all the app stores that support it.
    • Liquidity: You can buy or sell your Rimules through exchanges. The virtual currency in some app stores (e.g. Amazon gold) cannot be sold, only bought.
    • Earn coins: If a user clicks in an Ad, installs the app and gives two minutes of attention, he/she can earn coins to spend inside apps and games.

  • Rimule tokens can be used to obtain services on any app store that supports the Rimules protocol as well as to reward users. Some examples:

    • Developers can invest on advertising to promote their apps;
    • Users can be rewarded with Rimules by installing and using sponsored apps;
    • Developers can sell and users can buy digital goods using in-app billing;

  • Rimules can be purchased in selected exchange platforms or peer-to-peer from Rimule owners. Rimules shall be transferred and stored in a wallet.

  • Rimules is an ERC20 token, and can be stored in any ERC20 compatible wallet. We do not recommend a particular product, but some users report storing their Rimules using MyEtherWallet or imToken.

  • To participate in the Rimules token ICO sale you will need a wallet that is smart contract compatible. Please do not send your token directly from any cryptocurrency exchanges. You need a token compatible Ethereum wallet.

    The following wallets are known to be compatible with Ethereum tokens (ERC-20 standard):

    • MyEtherWallet (no download needed)
    • MetaMask (Firefox and Chrome browser addon)
    • Mist (Desktop)
    • Parity (Desktop)
    • Parity + Ledger (Hardware wallet)
    • imToken (iPhone)
    • imToken (Android)

  • Because Rimules is blockchain-based, and the Rimule tokens are ERC20 based, they can be traded from and to cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin or Ethereum and to fiat currencies. People can freely buy and sell these tokens, and many hold in anticipation of increased purchasing power for various Rimules related services.

  • You need to store the APPC tokens in ERC20 compliant wallets. With these you can transfer Rimules back and forth to your peers.
